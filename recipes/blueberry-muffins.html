Blueberry Muffins

Description

Delicious blueberry muffins with a crumble topping

Ingredients

Muffins

  • 1 12 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk (or more as needed)
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Crumb Topping

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinammon

Vodka Pasta

Description

A deliciously creamy pasta dish with a hint of spice.

This recipe ties in elements of heavy cream, parmesan cheese, red pepper and tomato to create a hearty and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 small clove of garlic (diced)
  • 1 small red onion (diced)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vodka
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 16-18oz Farfalle pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese (shredded)
  • Basil (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Take out a large saucepan and add olive oil
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat
  3. Add diced garlic and red onion to the pan and cook for 7 minutes, or until soft.
  4. Add tomato paste to the pan and cook until the paste darkens in color
  5. Add heavy cream
  6. Add red pepper flakes and stir until combined
  7. Sprinkle in desired amount of salt and pepper
  8. Remove sauce from heat
  9. Boil a pot of water adding salt to the water
  10. Cook pasta accoiding to direction
  11. Before draining pasta, set aside 1/2 cup of pasta water
  12. Place the sauce back on medium heat
  13. Add butter and stir until melted
  14. Add cooked pasta and pasta water
  15. Add parmesan cheese, salt and pepper and stir
  16. Serve immediately with flakes of basil
  17. Enjoy!

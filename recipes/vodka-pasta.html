Vodka Pasta

Description

A deliciously creamy pasta dish with a hint of spice.

This recipe ties in elements of heavy cream, parmesan cheese, red pepper and tomato to create a hearty and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 small clove of garlic (diced)
  • 1 small red onion (diced)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vodka
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 16-18oz Farfalle pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese (shredded)
  • Basil (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
